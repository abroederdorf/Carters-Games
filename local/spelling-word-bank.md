# Spelling Mode Word Bank

Target: 3-letter (Easy) and 4-letter (Medium) words for the fishing game spelling mode.

Two word pools:
- **Picture words** — shown with an illustration; playable with audio on or off
- **Audio-only words** — high-frequency sight words with no illustration; only cycled in when audio is ON (the spoken word is the only cue)

Words marked with ⚠️ may be harder to illustrate — consider moving them to the audio-only pool.

## Notes & Decisions

- **Audio-only pool**: Only shown when master audio is ON. If audio is muted, skip these and draw only from picture words.
- **Picture priority**: Pick words where the image is obvious (cat, cake, bike). Use a fun cartoon image.
- **Abstract words** (⚠️): Move to audio-only pool rather than skipping entirely.
- **Audio**: Pre-record all words clearly and slowly in a friendly voice. Sight words especially benefit from a warm, encouraging tone.
- **Word family display**: Consider showing the shared letters highlighted differently (e.g. _at ending in a different color) to reinforce the pattern.
- **Missing letter mechanic**: For Easy (3-letter), always the first or last letter goes on a fish. For Medium (4-letter), could be first, last, or middle letter.
